## Category Selection Tips

- Use the most specific category that fits — `cs.LG` for ML papers, not just `cs`.
- Cross-listed papers appear in multiple categories; the primary category is the first one listed.
- Many power electronics papers appear under `eess.PE` or `eess.SY`.
- Signal processing papers spanning EE and CS often appear in both `eess.SP` and `cs.LG`.
- When unsure, use `all:` field prefix with strong keywords instead of relying on category alone.
