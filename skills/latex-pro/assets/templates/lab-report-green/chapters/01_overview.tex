\subsection{Design Overview}
This laboratory report documents the design intent, evaluation criteria, and
technical reasoning for the circuit under test. It is structured to support
engineering review, traceability, and follow-up validation.

\textbf{Primary goals:}
\begin{itemize}
    \item explain the circuit purpose and boundaries;
    \item summarize major design choices and assumptions;
    \item record the most important sizing and validation results.
\end{itemize}
